Too good to be true?

With Nimbufy, you can recreate any design in just a few clicks! Try Nimbufy and experience the ease and convenience of web design like never before.

  • Can it convert HTML to Bricks/Oxygen compatible format?

    Yes, absolutely! Nimbufy transforms any HTML layout, complete with styling, into a format editable and customizable within Bricks Builder or Oxygen Builder.

  • Does it import JavaScript as well?

    Nimbufy is tuned primarily for HTML/CSS and does not import JavaScript.

  • Are there any limitations that I should know of?

    Nimbufy is in continuous development and is getting better by the day. For now, it would be safe to avoid importing slideshows, carousels and complex navigation menus. It may not be able to display imported icons in some cases, but there will be placeholders for the missing icons which you can easily replace with your own.

  • What does it cost?

    Nimbufy is available with a purchased license as a WordPress plugin, for both Oxygen Builder and Bricks Builder, granting you unlimited usage for converting webpages. The Chrome extension is free to install, and it works alongside our WordPress plugin(s) to provide seamless and unlimited conversions.

  • Can I try Nimbufy before purchasing?

    Yes! We offer a 5-day free trial with the monthly subscription plan.
