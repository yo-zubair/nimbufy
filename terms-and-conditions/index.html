Limitations

Nimbufy 3 is engineered to work with a broad range of HTML layouts, facilitating the seamless import of website components into your Oxygen Builder for WordPress. However, there are certain scenarios where Nimbufy may face difficulties, which we outline below in the interest of transparency and user satisfaction.

  1. Custom HTML Elements: While Nimbufy 3 supports most standard HTML elements, it may struggle with custom elements. Websites that use bespoke HTML elements or unconventional coding structures may not import as expected.
  2. Complex JavaScript: Nimbufy 3 can handle standard JavaScript usage but may encounter issues with complex or unique JavaScript implementations. If a website relies heavily on JavaScript for its structure or functionality, some parts may not be imported correctly.
  3. Nested Navigation Structures: We advise against importing nested navigation menus with Nimbufy 3. While the plugin can handle basic navigation structures, intricate or multi-layered menus may not import as intended.
  4. Slideshows and Carousels: Similar to nested navigation structures, slideshows and carousels have a complex structure that may present challenges during import. For optimal results, we recommend avoiding the import of these elements.
  5. Icons: In some cases, Nimbufy 3 may not display imported icons correctly. This limitation is particularly relevant if the icons are implemented using a custom font or a less common method.
  6. Other Scenarios: There may be other unforeseen scenarios where Nimbufy 3 encounters issues due to complex or non-standard website structures, unique CSS styling, or proprietary technologies used in a website. We encourage users to experiment and adapt their usage of Nimbufy 3 according to the specific characteristics of each website they work with. As always, our team is dedicated to improving the tool’s robustness and versatility, and we value your feedback in this ongoing process.

We are constantly working to enhance Nimbufy 3’s capabilities and hope to address these limitations in future versions. In the meantime, understanding these constraints will allow you to leverage Nimbufy 3 more effectively and shape your WordPress projects with confidence.
